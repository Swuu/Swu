Carnegie Mellon School of Music

A National Association of Schools of Music accredited school, the Carnegie Mellon School of Music offers undergraduate and graduate study, as well as Pre-College Program in the summer. Students receive the highest level of individualized instruction from professional musicians and master teachers.

Swu has worked with the president of Pittsburgh Digital Recording & Editing Company, Riccardo Shulz, Grammy Award-winning songwriter and producer, Aders Oland, Columbia-signed rapper FRZY, and Music Tech masters student Steven Krenn in the studio, music production, and live performances. All in all, the CMU School of Music has been an integral part of Swu's musical career.

Independent Musicians Organization

The Independent Musicians Organization (IMO) is an organization dedicated to connecting and promoting the musicians on the Carnegie Mellon campus! They run an annual festival to showcase Pittsburgh and campus talent during Carnival Weekend and serve as a hub for musicians looking for opportunities to play music & collaborate.

Swu has worked with IMO for years, writing and performing songs with many of their stellar musicians, including Varun Gadh and Ariana Mapua. They have collaborated at many popular college venues, such as Skibo Cafe, Sigma Nu, UC Jams, and Hack-a-song.

Boy Scouts of America

The Boy Scouts of America is one of the nation's largest and most prominent values-based youth development organizations. The BSA provides a program for young people that builds character, trains them in the responsibilities of participating citizenship, and develops personal fitness.

In 2010, Swu founded an annual charity concert called Project Warmth that officially partnered with Boy Scouts Troop 943. Project Warmth aims to raise awareness and support the local Chinese villagers through the harsh Beijing winters. After three successful years, Swu raised enough funds to install heating systems in several villages, consistently donated van-loads of warm clothing every year, and was awarded the ACAMIS Service Award and Grant.

Iron Horse

Iron Horse is a local bar and grill restaurant that also sells motorcycles. It specializes in barbequeue, burgers, and Harley-Davidson style badass motorcycles. Enjoy casual dining in an all American diner with live music every Friday night.

Swu has been featured in this restaurant on Friday nights for years. Playing a variety of rock, pop, jazz, and dance music for the folks enjoying their food.
