About Swu

Coming from The Golden State, Swu is one the the industry's most innovative recording artist and producer. Growing up and living all around the world, Swu embodies the creative and open-mindedness of different cultures. In his first album Human, he hopes bring to life emotions from the best parts of our lives to remind us that we are all human. He has stated that "music is a rare gift that crosses all language barriers and is universally understood. Each song captures an emotion and a story, and each story is from a part of my life."

Swu's music career started in the 4th grade, when he won a songwriting competition. He used notation software to write an electronic beat, and didn't even know he incorperated advanced techniques such as polyrithms, sus7 chords, and stereo imaging. Since then, Swu has picked up a myriad of different instruments, from the baritone, to drums, to guitar, and to voice. These were the first stepping stones to a lifetime career in music.

From his musical background and studying computer science at Carnegie Mellon, Swu is able to synthesize unique sounds and write avant garde chord and note progressions. He uses Nyquist to synthesize his own sounds, Carnegie Mellon's studio to record voice, and Logic Pro to put it all together. Throughout his musical career, he has been able to perform weekly sets at Iron Horse, a local bar and grill, perform in spring fairs, carnivals, and concert halls, and found an annual charity concert with the Boy Scouts of America. Now, he is focused on creating and producing his own music with the Carnegie Mellon School of Music, IMO, and independent artists.
