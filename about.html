About Swu

Hailing from The Golden State - San Francisco, CA - comes Swu, one of the industry's most versatile recording artists and producers. Growing up and living all around the world, Swu embodies the creative and open-mindedness of different cultures. In his first album Human, he hopes bring to life emotions from the best parts of our lives to remind us that we are all human. He has stated that "music is a rare gift that crosses all language barriers and is universally understood. Each song captures an emotion and a story, and each story is from a part of my life."

Swu's music career started in the 4th grade, when we won a music composition competition. He used a sound synthesis software to write a song called "juice box", named after the juice box he was drinking at the time. Out of over 30 students, his piece was selected and given the winning title. Since then, Swu has picked up a myriad of different instruments, from the baritone, to drums, to guitar, and to voice. These were the first stepping stones to a lifetime career in music.

From this musical background and studying computer science at Carnegie Mellon, Swu is able to synthesize unique sounds and write avant garde chord and note progressions. Throughout his musical career, he has been able to apply these skills in performing weekly sets in a local bar and grill, "Iron Horse", performing in spring fairs and carnivals, and founding an annual charity musical production with the Boy Scouts of America. Now, he is focusing on creating and producing his own music with the Carnegie Mellon Music Dept. and various independent artists.
